1. Windows Installation
- download and extract 4PairsofGlassesand12BadEyes.zip
- in the folder, you will see two files, GetGoingDesktop.exe* and GetGoingMobile.exe*

*if Windows prevents you from opening the files, click 'More info' and then 'Run anyway'.

2. Functionality
  With Get Going, users can do the following:
  - Create an account to Get Started
  - View nearby events and join them
  - Get matched with other users who are attending the same event
  - Sign in as a host and create a new event

3. A Brief Tutorial
- Get Started and fill out your information to register for an account.
- Be sure to fill out all of your interests. This is how you will be matched with other users!
- After you have registered you have the option to login as a regular user. If you are
on the desktop application, you can login as a host.
- After logging in as a user, you will see your personalized Event Stream at the top left,
and Interest Stream at the bottom left.
- To join an event, simply double click an event you see in your Interest Stream, after
clicking 'Refresh My Events', you will see the event show up in the Event Stream.
- To get matched with a date, double click on the event you want to get matched for. You
should recieve a message on whether or not the match was a success.
- To view your dates, click the 'See Dates' button on the right side of the application.

4. Things to Avoid
- no known issues
- Get Verified does not have full functionality
